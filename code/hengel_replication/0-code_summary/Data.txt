OPERATIONS
----------
- Loads variable and value labels from CSVs for use throughout the script
- Builds editorial cluster IDs from journal/volume/issue/part combinations
- Merges article metadata with author-article links and gender data
- Computes author career metrics: t5 (top-5 pub count at time of pub), T5 (lifetime), Maxt/MaxT (most experienced co-author)
- Derives gender composition variables: FemRatio, FemSenior, FemJunior, FemSolo, Fem100, Fem50, Fem1
- Computes 5-year moving-average institutional ranks and bins them into InstRank buckets
- Generates publication order within journal issues
- Classifies articles as Theory/Empirical/Other from JEL codes
- Creates primary JEL one-hot dummies (JEL1_*) and tertiary JEL dummies (JEL3_*)
- Merges readability statistics and computes derived text metrics (words/sentence, polysyllables/word, etc.)
- Computes LLM group composite scores G1-G5 from 15 individual LLM criteria; all 15 individual criteria AND the 5 composites AND llm_readability are retained in all article and NBER datasets
- Generates article-level datasets with/without P&P and with/without JEL dummies; all carry the full set of LLM variables
- Builds author-level panel datasets for top-4 and top-5 journals with career position, author weights, and factor variable bases; author datasets inherit LLM variables from article via merge
- Imports NBER working paper text stats, merges with article data, computes NBER-level LLM group scores; all 15 individual NBER criteria AND 5 composites AND nber_llm_readability are retained in all NBER datasets
- Reshapes NBER data into paired-difference (FE) format pairing NBER vs. published scores; nber_fe automatically includes D._llm_g1_score through D._llm_g5_score via the @_score reshape
- Builds review duration dataset merging timeline data with article and author characteristics; includes _flesch_score, _llm_readability_score, and _llm_g1_score through _llm_g5_score from article
