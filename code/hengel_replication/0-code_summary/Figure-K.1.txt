OPERATIONS
----------
- Merges review duration data with author-level career data
- Generates motherhood and childbirth indicators (child aged <= 4 at submission and acceptance)
- Estimates separate SURE regressions for junior (t==1) and senior (t>1) female authors
- Computes difference in review time between junior and senior women with confidence intervals
- Creates a coefplot with 90% CI comparing junior vs. senior women's review times
