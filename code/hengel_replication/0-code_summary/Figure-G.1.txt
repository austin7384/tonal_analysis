INPUTS
------
data/raw/hengel_generated/nber_fe.dta

OPERATIONS
----------
- Creates a continuous blind-review policy change variable centered at the volume where the policy changed
- For each of 6 readability measures (Flesch, Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, SMOG, Dale-Chall, LLM Readability): residualizes readability changes after controlling for covariates; estimates separate regressions for gender and seniority groups; uses margins to plot predicted changes by distance from the policy-change point
- Compares double-blind (pre-internet) vs. single-blind (post-internet) periods
- Creates a combined graph with a policy-change annotation line
- Exports a standalone Flesch graph and a combined graph of the remaining 5 measures (Flesch-Kincaid, Gunning Fog, SMOG, Dale-Chall, LLM Readability)

OUTPUTS
-------
outputs/figures/Figure-G.1-flesch.pdf
outputs/figures/Figure-G.1-combo.pdf
